Park Protection Success Stories

View at New River Gorge National Park, in Fayetteville, WV.

The Great American Outdoors Act has funded restoration projects across the National Park System at parks across the country. From historic structure maintenance and campground revitalization, to ecosystem protections and shoreline stabilization, GAOA funding has allowed projects of all sizes and scope to be completed.
